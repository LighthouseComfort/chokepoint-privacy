Chokepoint Privacy Policy

Effective: April 18, 2026

Chokepoint (the "app") is developed by Lighthouse Comfort. This policy describes what information the app does and does not handle.

The short version

Chokepoint does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Everything the app does happens on your device.

Data the app does not collect

  • No account creation, sign-in, or user identifiers
  • No analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or usage tracking
  • No advertising identifiers or ad networks
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access
  • No personal information is sent to Lighthouse Comfort or any third party

Data the app stores locally on your device

The following is saved only on your phone, in the app's private storage, and is never transmitted:

  • App settings and preferences
  • Saved pressure-test records and measurement history you create
  • Job information fields you enter (project name, address, notes, etc.)
  • Stats counters used by in-app achievements

Uninstalling the app or clearing its storage removes all of this data.

Device sensors and features the app uses

  • Barometric pressure sensor — for pressure differential measurements. Readings are displayed, optionally recorded locally, and never transmitted.
  • Motion sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope) — for gesture-based flashlight toggles, stabilization detection, and pipe pitch/level features. Motion data is processed in real time and not stored.
  • Camera flash (LED) — for the flashlight utility. The camera itself is not accessed.
  • Battery state — for battery-aware behavior in some themes and overlays. Read-only, not transmitted.

Export and sharing

You can export measurement data as PDF or CSV files. When you export, the file is generated on your device and handed to the Android share sheet, where you choose what to do with it (save, email, send to another app, etc.). Lighthouse Comfort receives no copy.

External links

The model-number lookup feature launches your device's default web browser with a search query you selected. Once you leave Chokepoint, you are subject to the privacy policy of whichever search engine you chose. Chokepoint does not proxy, log, or observe these searches.

Children

Chokepoint is a professional tool intended for adults working in HVAC and related trades. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
